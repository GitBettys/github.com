    My story:

    Hi loves, I want to tell you how Betty's natural beauty came about.


    It all started with serving one or another customer after work or school.


    Little by little I was passing courses, training myself with the studies and buying my tools.


    All the income I had was to invest in my products and in my business.


    Then I specialized in skin care, eyebrows and makeup.


    Meanwhile, surprising my clients with little gifts, even if I attended them in my living room.


    I started working in a doctor - spa for a year and a half; Collection of knowledge and practices. Also my clients at home.
